About Us

Korean.wish was founded by travel enthusiasts who want to promote Korea travel with affordable and locals recommended free and easy travel products. The need for such service was discovered when helping personal foreign friends planning their trip to Korea, realized that Korea is not free and easy friendly for foreigners due to language barrier, online payment policy issues etc… even though the Korean wave (Hallyu) is very popular.

Korean.wish Easy makes traveling in Korea easy and enjoyable, with foreign-friendly services including things to do activities, entertainment admission tickets, private car, guide, airport transport, accommodation, tours and more!.

Korean.wish also keeps you up to date with the hottest places and good travel information for Seoul and several places in Korea!.
